Meet Your Instructor

Welcome! I am a professional Kathak exponent and choreographer with over 15 years of experience in classical and semi-classical forms. My mission is to preserve the rich heritage of Indian dance while making it accessible to modern enthusiasts.

Our studio specializes in the Lucknow Gharana style, focusing on intricate footwork (Tatkar), lightning-fast pirouettes (Chakkars), and evocative expressions (Abhinaya).
